TECHPHILIA IS AN ONLINE TECHNICAL FEST WHICH IS TO BE ORGANISED BY THE STUDENTS OF BCA 5TH SEMESTER AND MCA 5TH SEMESTER STUDENTS (AIIT, PATNA).

BRIEF ABOUT THE EVENT:

AS THE NAME SUGGESTS THE EVENT WILL CELEBRATE AND APPRECIATE PEOPLE’S LOVE FOR TECHNOLOGY. THIS FEST IS NOT ONLY FOR AMITY BUT ALSO FOR STUDENTS ALL AROUND THE GLOBE. STUDENTS FROM TECHNICAL BACKGROUND AS WELL AS NON-TECHNICAL STUDENTS BUT HAVING FASCINATION FOR TECHNOLOGY ARE CORDIALLY INVITED TO BE A PART OF THE FEST. THEY CAN ALSO PARTICIPATE IN THE VARIOUS EVENTS THAT ARE GOING TO BE ORGANISED. WE WILL HAVE A LOT OF FUN.
